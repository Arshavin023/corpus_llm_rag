Company Vehicle & Test Drive Policy

Test Drive Procedures

Before any test drive, the Sales Consultant must:

  • Scan a valid Driver's License into the system.
  • Verify the customer is at least 21 years of age.
  • Ensure the "Trade-In" vehicle is parked in the designated security zone.

Demonstrator (Demo) Vehicles

Senior managers assigned a Demo vehicle are responsible for its cleanliness. Smoking or transporting pets in Demo units is strictly prohibited. Mileage must be logged weekly via the Employee Portal.
