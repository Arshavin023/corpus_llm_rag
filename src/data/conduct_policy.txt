APEX MOTORS: CODE OF CONDUCT & REMOTE WORK POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. PROFESSIONAL ETHICS
All sales consultants and technicians must maintain the highest level of integrity. 
Misrepresenting vehicle history or pricing is strictly prohibited. 

2. REMOTE WORK ELIGIBILITY
Administrative and digital marketing staff are eligible for "Hybrid-Flex" status. 
Sales staff must be present on the showroom floor during assigned shifts. 
Remote work requires a stable internet connection (min 25Mbps) and a 
distraction-free environment.

3. DATA SECURITY
Employees working remotely must use the company-provided VPN to access 
the inventory management system. Sharing login credentials for the 
Dealer Management System (DMS) results in immediate termination.
