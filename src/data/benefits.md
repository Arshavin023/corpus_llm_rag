# Apex Motors Benefits & Leave Policy

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
Employees accrue PTO based on years of service:
* **0-2 Years:** 10 days per year
* **3-5 Years:** 15 days per year
* **5+ Years:** 20 days per year

## Holiday Schedule
Apex Motors is closed on the following days:
1. New Year's Day
2. Independence Day
3. Thanksgiving Day
4. Christmas Day

**Note:** The service department may have "On-Call" rotations for emergency roadside 
assistance during holidays. These hours are paid at 1.5x the standard rate.

## Requesting Leave
All leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least **14 days** in advance. 
Blackout dates apply during the "Year-End Sales Event" (Dec 20 - Jan 2).
