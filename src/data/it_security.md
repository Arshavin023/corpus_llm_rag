# Apex Motors IT Security Policy

## Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol
- Passwords expire every 90 days

## Device Usage
- Only company-issued devices may access internal systems
- USB storage devices are prohibited unless approved by IT

## Incident Reporting
All suspected breaches must be reported within **24 hours** to IT security.

## Email Safety
Employees must not click unknown links or download suspicious attachments.
